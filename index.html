Spreading Awareness

Several factors have contributed to burgeoning issues of environmental degradation and climate change that we face today. The unruly disposal of e-waste contributes significantly to the same and has resulted in very poor air quality. Our aim is to spread awareness on clean, and more sustainable methods of e-waste disposal to protect the environment.
